Two suites, one app

Version 2.0 adds a complete construction management subsystem alongside the real-estate toolkit.

⌂Real Estate

  • ▸Property listings with regional units (Marla, Kanal, Sq.Ft, Sq.M)
  • ▸Project / housing-society layout with blocks, streets and plots
  • ▸Payment plans with optional KIBOR / benchmark-linked variable rates
  • ▸Bookings, invoicing and receivables aging via Odoo Accounting
  • ▸Investor deals, dealership and commission management
  • ▸Property transfers with country-aware taxes and transfer deed
  • ▸Buyback & surrender wizards, document management, QR verification
  • ▸Live dashboard for bookings, receivables and plan status

⚒Construction

  • ▸Construction projects broken into phases with progress tracking
  • ▸Subcontracts with a detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ)
  • ▸Interim Payment Certificates (IPC) certifying executed quantities
  • ▸Material requisitions that raise Purchase RFQs automatically
  • ▸Material issues to site with native Inventory stock moves
  • ▸Project cost model on Analytic Accounting and Timesheets
  • ▸Certified-vs-contract tracking to control over-billing
◉

Country-aware tax engine

Define transfer taxes — stamp duty, registration, capital gains, VAT/GST, withholding, land-department fees — and miscellaneous charges per country, then simply pick your operating country in Configuration. Ready-made presets ship for several markets and you can add your own. No hard-coded country logic.

Built natively on Odoo Accounting, Sales, Inventory, Purchase, Project, Timesheets, CRM, Discuss, Portal, Products & Contacts.
