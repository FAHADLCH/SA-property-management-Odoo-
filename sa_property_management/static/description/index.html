SA Property Management

Real Estate ERP, built for Pakistan

Manage housing societies, plots, apartments, payment plans, dealerships and transfers — all with CVT, Stamp Duty, FBR and DC-value logic baked in.

Everything a Pakistani real-estate office needs

🏘 Project & Property Listing

Group properties by housing society / sector / block. Native support for Marla, Kanal, Sq.Ft, Sq.Yard and Sq.Meter.

📅 Payment Plans

Down payment + N installments (monthly / quarterly / yearly) + balloon payments + on-possession charge — preview before confirming.

💸 Receivables Management

Automatic installment schedule, integrated customer invoicing, overdue aging and one-click payment registration.

🤝 Dealership Management

Track dealers/agents, configurable commission %, auto-calculated sales and earned commission per dealer.

🔄 Property Transfer

Owner-change workflow with CVT, Stamp Duty, Registration Fee, FBR 236C/236K, CGT and full miscellaneous-charge support — all data-driven, all postable to Odoo Accounting.

⚙ Fully Configurable Backend

Every tax, charge, journal, account and default UoM lives in Settings. Zero hard-coding, easy to localize to your city/society.

Built on Odoo core

Leverages account for invoicing, mail for follow-ups, portal for self-service, and product for catalog patterns. No external Python dependencies. Tested on Odoo 18 (Community & Enterprise).

Reports out of the box

  • Booking Confirmation (with CNIC, property details, plan summary)
  • Payment Schedule (full installment list with paid/residual)
  • Transfer Deed (transferor / transferee, taxes, misc charges, net to seller)
