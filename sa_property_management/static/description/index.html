Real estate and construction in one app

Version 2.0 adds a full construction management suite alongside the real-estate toolkit.

Real estate

  • Property listings with regional units (Marla, Kanal, Sq.Ft, Sq.M)
  • Payment plans with optional KIBOR / benchmark-linked variable rates
  • Bookings, invoicing and receivables aging via Odoo Accounting
  • Investor deals, dealership and commission management
  • Property transfers with country-aware taxes and transfer deed
  • Buyback & surrender wizards, document management and QR verification

Construction

  • Construction projects broken into phases with progress tracking
  • Subcontracts with a detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ)
  • Interim Payment Certificates (IPC) for certified quantities
  • Material requisitions that raise Purchase RFQs automatically
  • Material issues to site with native Inventory stock moves
  • Project cost model on Analytic Accounting and Timesheets

Single codebase verified on Odoo 18.0 and 19.0 — 49 automated tests pass on both series.
